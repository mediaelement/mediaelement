MediaElement.js is a blazingly fast and amazingly powerful HTML5 audio and video library that creates a unified feel for all media files (MP4, WebM, MP3, FLV), streaming content (HLS, M(PEG)-DASH, RTMP), and embeddable players like YouTube, Vimeo, Twitch, DailyMotion, Facebook, and SoundCloud.
